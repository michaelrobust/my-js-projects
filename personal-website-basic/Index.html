Biography

Hello! I'm Michael, a computer science student with a passion for web development and artificial intelligence. I grew up in San Francisco, where I developed my love for technology by tinkering with computers and learning programming languages in my spare time.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy exploring the city's diverse neighborhoods, trying new restaurants, and hiking in the nearby mountains.
